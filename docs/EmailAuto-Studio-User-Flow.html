Step 0 · Access

Sign in & approval

What you do
  1. Open the app → land on the sign-in screen (Crossian logo).
  2. New user: Sign up with email + password.
  3. Existing approved user: Sign in and go straight to the studio.
What happens
  • New signups start as pending
    "awaiting admin approval" screen
  • An admin approves you → full access
    deactivated accounts are blocked
  • Session persists; you return straight to the studio

Step 1 · Brief

Describe the campaign

BriefProductsPromptsCopyPreviewExport
What you do
  1. Pick the brand (BraGoddess / GentsLux / LuxFitting / SantaFare).
  2. Set send date and the recipient name token.
  3. Choose tiers (A–F lifecycle) and product segments.
  4. Write the offer and an optional Hook Contract.
What you get
  • The variant matrix is set
    tiers × segments (e.g. A21, F8…)
  • Brand voice, hero product & rules pre-loaded
  • The Hook Contract becomes the single source of truth for all copy

Step 2 · Products

Choose the products

BriefProductsPromptsCopyPreviewExport
What you do
  1. Pick the products to feature from the brand's catalog.
  2. The hero product is locked into position 1.
  3. Keep it to ≤ 6 products (the proven sweet spot).
What you get
  • A clean, on-brand product set
    hero always leads the email
  • Prices & slugs feed the copy + links automatically
  • Guardrail warns if you exceed 6 products

Step 3 · Prompts

Review the prompts before they're sent

BriefProductsPromptsCopyPreviewExport
What you do
  1. See the exact system prompt (brand + persona) and one user prompt per tier.
  2. Edit any of them if you want — copy or reset per prompt.
  3. Click Generate when you're happy.
What you get
  • Full transparency — what you see is what's sent
  • Fine control over tone/instructions per tier
  • Confidence before spending an API call

Step 4 · Copy

Generate & refine the copy

BriefProductsPromptsCopyPreviewExport
What you do
  1. Hit Generate — one Claude call per tier produces every variant.
  2. Switch between variants (A21, F8…) via tabs.
  3. Inline-edit subject, preheader, body, CTA; Regenerate if needed.
What you get
  • On-brand, persona-written copy per variant
    named micro-story, single hook, on-brand voice
  • Strict structure (subject ≤ chars, preheader, CTA)
  • Everything editable before preview

Step 5 · Preview

Preview, add images & QA

BriefProductsPromptsCopyPreviewExport
What you do
  1. See the email render live; toggle mobile / desktop width.

EmailAuto Studio · User Flow09
Anytime · History

Reopen a saved campaign

What you do
  1. Click 🕘 History in the top bar.
  2. Browse your saved generations (name, variants, date).
  3. Open to restore, or Delete.
What you get
  • The whole campaign restored into the studio
    brief, all variants, images — back to Preview
  • Re-export or re-sync without redoing the work
  • Private to you (Row-Level Security)
EmailAuto Studio · User Flow10
Admins only · Admin

Manage the team

What you do
  1. Click 🛠 Admin (visible to admins only).
  2. Approve pending signups.
  3. Activate / deactivate users or reset a password.
What you get
  • Controlled access — only approved users get in
  • Instant enable/disable of teammates
  • Secure: admin actions run server-side, can't self-deactivate
