T  ·  Task

Root Causes & Required Actions

Root Cause 1 — List expansion: SF and LF CTR fell when March delivery scaled into colder addresses. Scale only after the previous send holds CTR.

Root Cause 2 — Body fatigue: Repeated micro-story → product → proof → CTA arcs are now recognizable. Rotate opener mechanics every send.

Root Cause 3 — Weak hook coherence: Cold readers need subject, preheader, banner, and first paragraph to carry one clear reason to click.

  • Rotate opener type every send: story, fact, question, occasion.
  • Keep one hook across subject, preheader, banner, and body.
  • Relaunch SF in Q4 with VIP and active buyers first.
  • If Send 4 CTR drops >15% vs Send 1, pause scale-up.
  • Keep hero products, but change the pain/relief story.

Part 2 — Prompt System

Use the same five-step chain for every brand. Each prompt has one job, a clear output shape, and explicit failure rules.

Do

  • Choose one angle before writing.
  • Lead with a proven hero product.
  • Put one inline product link by paragraph 2.
  • Write subject + preheader as one pair.

Don't

  • Stack birthday + season + discount + countdown.
  • Open with bullets, checkmarks, or generic gratitude.
  • Hide price until the product grid.
  • Repeat "Customer Reviews + Thank You" as the monthly default.
